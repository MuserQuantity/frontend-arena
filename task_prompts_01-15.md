## 任务 8（task_07）：Realistic lava lamp simulation

Build a realistic lava lamp simulation using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The simulation should model the behavior of wax blobs rising and falling inside a liquid due to heat, gravity, and buoyancy forces. The blobs should:

Rise when heated, then cool and fall when they reach the top.
Merge and separate dynamically when blobs collide, mimicking real fluid behavior.
Simulate gravity pulling the blobs down and heat pushing them up.
Include random variations in blob sizes and movement for a natural, chaotic effect.
Make the blobs stretch, deform, and wobble as they move, split, or combine.
Use physics and viscosity effects to simulate the liquid environment inside the lamp.
The heat force will decrease with distance from bottom, try and keep the total volume/area of the blobs constant.
Sliders to adjust constants, the blobs should have only 1 color and have them randomly spawn every once in a while.

## 任务 11（task_10）：Gamepad rhythm game with arc-shaped note flicking

Create a simple rhythm game utilizing a gamepad. Arc-shaped notes should rhythmically gravitate towards the center from random directions. The player must flick the stick in the direction of the note to hit it right as the arc-shaped note hits a small circle in the middle. The timing window should be +-200 milliseconds. The arc of the note should be 60 degrees. Nominally, at least one note should fall every second, but rhythm patterns should be somewhat varied. Each note should be visible on the screen for approximately one second.
